Who We Are

Bridging Global Healthcare Supply Chains

At Panaurex, we don't just trade products; we deliver solutions. We act as a vital link in the global healthcare supply chain, ensuring that essential medical and veterinary supplies reach the markets that need them most.

Our Mission

To empower global healthcare by providing seamless access to high-quality pharmaceutical and veterinary solutions. We strive to improve life and livestock health through the ethical and efficient distribution of medicines, chemicals, and advanced medical technology.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted global partner in healthcare trade, recognized for our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and the enhancement of well-being across borders.

Services & Operations

Comprehensive Healthcare Trading & Global Logistics

We provide a 360-degree service model that simplifies the complexity of international healthcare trade. Our operations are designed to ensure product integrity, regulatory compliance, and timely delivery.

01

Global Export & Distribution

We specialize in navigating the complexities of international trade. From managing cross-border documentation to adhering to country-specific health regulations, we ensure a smooth flow of medical and veterinary supplies to our global partners.

02

Sourcing & Procurement

Leveraging a vast network of certified manufacturers, we source high-grade APIs, pharmaceuticals, and veterinary supplements. We act as your eyes on the ground, ensuring every batch meets the required specifications before it enters the supply chain.

03

Quality Assurance & Compliance

Quality is our North Star. We offer rigorous vetting of all products, ensuring they carry the necessary certifications (such as GMP or ISO) required for international markets. We handle the compliance heavy lifting so you don't have to.

04

Veterinary Portfolio Management

Beyond human medicine, we offer specialized consulting and supply services for the veterinary industry, helping farmers and clinics optimize animal health with our "Pan" branded supplements and feed additives.

05

Turnkey Healthcare Equipment Supply

We provide end-to-end solutions for medical facilities looking to upgrade their technology, offering procurement and logistics for advanced diagnostic and surgical equipment.
